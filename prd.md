Overview

DonateConnect is a mobile and web platform that connects people who want to donate items with those in need. The app streamlines item listing, discovery, and engagement, making donating and receiving goods easy, safe, and efficient.

Core Features
1. Item Donation Flow
Category & Subcategory Selection: Donors choose an item type (e.g., Clothing, Electronics, Furniture) and a more specific subcategory.
Item Details: Add a short description and optional photo(s) of the item
Location & Contact: Pin the location for pickup/drop-off and provide preferred contact details.
Post & Share: Publish the item to the platform. Option to share externally via social media or messaging apps.
2. Dashboard / Item Discovery
Real-time Feed: Recipients can browse items by category, subcategory, or proximity.
Map View: Visualize nearby available items using an interactive map.
Filters & Search: Filter by item type, distance, condition, or donation urgency.
3. Engagement & Pickup
Request or Reserve: Interested users can request an item directly through the app.
Messaging: Secure in-app messaging allows donors and recipients to coordinate pickup.
Confirmation: Mark items as collected or still available.
4. Social & Community Features
Donor/Recipient Profiles: Track past donations or pickups to build trust.
Reputation System: Ratings or badges for donors and recipients to encourage reliability.
Notifications: Alert users when relevant items are posted nearby or when requests are received.
5. Administrative & Analytics
Dashboard Analytics: Track item categories, engagement levels, popular locations, and donation trends.
Moderation Tools: Ensure posted items meet community guidelines.
Value Proposition
For Donors: Easily declutter and make a positive impact with minimal effort.
For Recipients: Quickly discover needed items in their area without unnecessary hurdles.
For Communities: Foster a culture of giving while reducing waste and promoting reuse.
